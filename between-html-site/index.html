Founding Membership

Founding Membership is membership of The Space Between community the people who show up, year-round, and build the case together that Burton deserves more.

It is not membership of a venue. A permanent home for grassroots music in Burton is our ambition. We're working to build the business case with funders, the council, and the community. Every Founding Member is evidence that the demand is real.

Back the year-round programme. Back the campaign. Be one of the first people to say: Burton is serious about this.

The ambition

Burton hasn't had a dedicated grassroots music venue for a long time.

East Staffordshire Borough Council is exploring the business case for one. Our ambition is to be part of making it happen if the demand is there.

The Space Between is how we find out, together, whether it is. Every event we run, every person who joins the community, every voice added here builds the picture funders and decision-makers need to see.

We're not there yet. But we're building the case and you're part of it.

Access

The Space Between is for everyone. Here's what we have in place and how to tell us what you need.

♿

Step-free access

All venues on our programme have step-free access. Access details are listed per event.

🔇

Quiet spaces

Quiet areas available at indoor events. Let us know in advance and we'll confirm what's available.

🤟

BSL interpretation

BSL interpretation available on request. Contact us at least two weeks before the event.

🎟

Access fund & Tickets for Good

Complimentary companion tickets available. We're proud to be a Tickets for Good partner.

We are committed to the Safe Gigs for Women and Good Night Out Campaign frameworks.
